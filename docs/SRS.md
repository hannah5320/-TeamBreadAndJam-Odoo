# Software Requirements Specification

## Project

AssetFlow

Enterprise Asset and Resource Management System

---

## Objective

Develop a centralized web application for managing company assets, employees, departments, bookings, maintenance requests, audits and notifications.

---

- Department Management
- Employee Management
- Asset Category Management
- Asset Management

- Resource Booking
- Maintenance Tracking
- Audit Tracking
- Notifications
